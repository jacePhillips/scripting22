Most people who work in Airdrie work for small family businesses or schools. For the rest, work in Calgary and use Airdrie to live without the concern of working. With the addition of new communities being added Airdires strong evergrowing community is bustling with new growth on the daily with more people coming to visit and even more coming and making it a place, they can call home. Airdrie's economy has never been stronger and shows no sign of slowing down any time soon. With the new addition of updated and new parks for the whole family to enjoy with brand new houses just around the block you can not only enjoy a nice walk around a new community but if you so please you might even find a place that you might someday call home.
